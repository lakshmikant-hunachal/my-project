Essential Crop Knowledge

Understand the fundamental crop seasons and their requirements.

🌾

Kharif Crops

Season: Monsoon (July - October)

Require warm, wet weather. Major crops include Rice, Maize, Sorghum, Pearl Millet/Bajra, Finger Millet/Ragi (cereals), Arhar (pulses), Soyabean.

🌱

Rabi Crops

Season: Winter (October - March)

Need cold weather for growth and warm weather for seed maturation. Key crops are Wheat, Barley, Oats (cereals), Chickpea/Gram (pulses), Linseed, Mustard.

🍉

Zaid Crops

Season: Summer (March - June)

Grown in the short season between Kharif and Rabi. These require warm, dry weather and a longer day length for flowering. Examples: Watermelon, Cucumber, Bitter Gourd.

Common Crop Diseases

Identify and treat devastating plant diseases before they spread.

Leaf Blight

Cause: Fungal or Bacterial pathogens

Characterized by rapid browning and dying of plant tissue. Often thrives in warm, wet conditions.

Fungicide Treatment

Powdery Mildew

Cause: Fungal spores

Appears as white, powdery spots on leaves and stems. Can severely reduce crop yield by inhibiting photosynthesis.

Sulfur Sprays

Rust

Cause: Pucciniales Fungi

Identified by rust-colored spores on lower leaf surfaces. Extremely damaging to cereal crops like wheat.

Modern Farming Practices

💧

Precision Irrigation

Utilize drip or sprinkler systems to minimize water waste and deliver moisture directly to the root zone, improving water efficiency by over 30%.

🌍

Soil Conservation

Adopt cover cropping and no-till farming to prevent soil erosion, retain moisture, and build healthy soil organic matter essential for resilience.

🌿

Integrated Pest Management (IPM)

Combine biological, cultural, physical, and chemical tools in a way that minimizes economic, health, and environmental risks while suppressing pests.
